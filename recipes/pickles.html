Best Homemade Refrigerator Pickles

How to make the best refrigerator pickle recipe with no canning.

These pickles are very easy to make, and don't take much time at all to do. I'm sure you will enjoy them if you give them a try. Yumm!!!

There's nothing like home cooked food. This is also a great way to preserve your cucumber harvest if you have a home garden. I love pickles, if you do too give it a try!

Ingredients

  • Pickling Cucumbers
  • Vidalia Onion
  • Fresh Dill Weed
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Water
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Pickling Salt
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Whole Black Peppercorns
  • Whole Yellow Mustard Seeds
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Pack a clean pint-sized jar with sliced cucumbers, onion slices, and dill sprigs. Leave a 1/2 inch of space at the top of the jar for liquid.
  2. In a small saucepan heat the vinegar, water, garlic, and all spices until the mixture comes to a simmer and the salt and sugar dissolve.
  3. Cool the brine down to warm, and fill the jar so everything is covered with brine.
  4. Close the lid tightly and refrigerate for 24 hours before eating.
